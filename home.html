Home Interior Designs

We, DesignHarvest, are into designing houses . We like changes. We like creating things out of nothing.

Decorative Mindset

The essence of interior design will always be about people and how they live. It is about the realities of what makes for an attractive, civilized, meaningful environment, not about fashion or what's in or whats's out.

" Be faithful to your own taste, because nothing you really like is ever out of style. "

Choose What you love

Your home should tell the story of who you are, and be a collection of what you love. Don't be afraid of experiment - not everything has to match. A house that is 100% perfect is rarely full of charm.

" A room should never allow the eye to settle in one place. It should smile at you and create fantasy. "
